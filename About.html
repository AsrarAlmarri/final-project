Health Tracker is a website that helps age from 15 to 80 years old to calculate their daily requirment of calories from each group of food and facilitate their calculation by converting into grams. The site also contains examples from all food categories and each item has a specific portion size and macronutrient content. This site may also help patients with type 2 diabetes by helping them to know the servings of carbohydrates that are being consumed to control blood sugar and insulin doses. The menu is based on EXCHANGE LIST system.





What do The exchange lists do?
it group food together which are alike. Food on each list have about the same amount of carbohydrate, protein, fat and calories. In the amounts given, all choices on each list are equal. Any food on the list can be exchanged or traded for any other food on the list.
